# 1000 Company Proposal

## Goal
Build a database of 1000 ICP-qualified industrial companies within one month.

## Week 1
Build a universe of 5000+ companies using:
- LinkedIn Sales Navigator
- IndiaMART
- industrial directories
- automation ecosystems
- export databases

## Week 2
Use AI-assisted filtering to:
- identify manufacturers
- detect industrial specialization
- identify growth signals
- classify Industry 4.0 relevance

## Week 3
Manual validation:
- manufacturing verification
- ownership checks
- technical differentiation checks
- growth-signal validation

## Week 4
Quality control and enrichment:
- remove false positives
- enrich personalization hooks
- finalize Federer scoring
- prepare outreach-ready database

## Expected Yield
5000 researched
→ 2500 filtered
→ 1500 validated
→ 1000 final ICP-qualified companies

## Quality Control
- Cross-verification with official websites
- LinkedIn hiring validation
- Manual review of borderline companies
- Rejection of unsupported AI assumptions
