# AI Prompts Used

## Example Prompts
- Identify Indian industrial automation companies aligned with Industry 4.0
- Find process instrumentation manufacturers in India
- List industrial IoT companies with manufacturing relevance
- Do not invent certifications or unsupported claims
- Mention uncertainty where data is unavailable
- Use only evidence-based industrial information

## Hallucination Prevention
- Cross-verified AI outputs with company websites
- Compared multiple AI outputs
- Rejected unsupported assumptions
- Used manual validation for industrial relevance
