# Methodology

## Objective
The objective was to identify industrial automation and instrumentation companies aligned with the Federer framework.

## Research Process
1. Selected industrial sensors and instrumentation as the target segment.
2. Shortlisted companies through LinkedIn, industrial directories, automation ecosystems, and company websites.
3. Verified manufacturing capability, industrial relevance, and growth signals.
4. Evaluated companies using the Federer scoring framework.

## Hallucination Control
To reduce AI hallucination:
- company claims were manually verified
- websites were cross-checked
- industrial relevance was validated
- unsupported assumptions were rejected
- multiple AI outputs were compared before inclusion

## Key Learning
A major challenge was differentiating technically strong multinational automation firms from promoter-led Indian manufacturing organizations better aligned with the Federer ICP.
