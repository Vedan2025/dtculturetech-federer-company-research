# DeepThought Business Analytics Assignment

This assignment focuses on identifying “Federer companies” — technically differentiated, growth-oriented manufacturing organizations aligned with DeepThought’s Scientific Execution framework.

## Contents
- Final 25 company research sheet
- Rejected companies analysis
- Methodology document
- 1000-company scale-up proposal
- AI prompting and hallucination-control workflow
- Hand-drawn sourcing and qualification diagram

## Key Research Areas
- Industrial automation
- Process instrumentation
- Smart manufacturing
- IIoT and predictive maintenance
- Factory automation systems

## Research Methodology
Companies were identified using LinkedIn, industrial directories, company websites, automation ecosystems, and Industry 4.0 research.

AI tools were used for:
- structured research support
- comparative analysis
- company summarization
- scoring assistance

All outputs were manually validated to reduce hallucination and unsupported assumptions.
